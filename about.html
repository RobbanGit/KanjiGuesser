About

Kanji Guesser is a program that gives you the opportunity to practice your awesome and amazing kanji skills. Note that Kanji Guesser is just a side project, and progress/updates may stop at any time. It is by no means perfect, and may have plenty of design imperfections. Design is not my strong point. I take no responsibility whatsoever regarding issues that may or may not occur when you are using Kanji Guesser.

The kanji JSON dataset source is taken from davidluzgouveia (https://github.com/davidluzgouveia)'s repository: kanji-data (https://github.com/davidluzgouveia/kanji-data).

Kanji Guesser is built using ElectronJS.

Version: 0.1 (2022-02-12)
